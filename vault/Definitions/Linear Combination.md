
Definition:
A `linear combination` of a list $v_1, ..., v_m$ of vectors in V is a vector of the form:
$a_1 v_1 + ... + a_m v_m$
Where $a_1, ..., a_m \in \mathbb{F}$

## Example

(17, -4, 2) is a `linear combination` of (2, 1, -3) and (1, -2, 4) because

(17, -4, 2) = 6(2, 1, -3) + 5(1, -2, 4)

or:
- 17 = 6(2) + 5(1)
- -4 = 6(1) + 5(-2)
- 2 = 6(-3) + 5(4)
