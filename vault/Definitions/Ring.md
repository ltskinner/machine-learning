
## Definition

A set + 2 operations (usually addition and multiplication)

For the [[Addition]] [[Operation]]:
- [[Closed]]
- [[Associativity]]
- Additive [[Identity Element]]
- [[Additive Inverse]]
- [[Commutativity]]

For Multiplication Operation

For interactions between Addition and Multiplication:
- Multiplication distributes over addition ([[Distributive Property]])


## Intuition

Can freely perform:
- Addition and Multiplication

Not guaranteed to divide

## Examples

- $\mathbb{Z}$ (integers)
- Polynomials
- Matrices
