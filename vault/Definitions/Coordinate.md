
## Definition

$\mathbb{F}^n$ is the [[Set]] of all [[List]]s of elements of a [[Field]] $\mathbb{F}$

$\mathbb{F}^n = \{ (x_1, ..., x_n) : x_k \in \mathbb{F}$ for $k = 1, ..., n \}$

For $(x_1, ..., x_n) \in \mathbb{F}^n$ and $k \in \{1, ..., n \}$ we say that $x_k$ is the kth `coordinate` of $(x_1, ..., x_n)$
