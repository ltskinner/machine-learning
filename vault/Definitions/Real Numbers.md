Real Numbers ($\mathbb{R}$)

`real number` - the real numbers $\mathbb{R}$ form a continuous number line containing all rational and irrational numbers

## Intuition

$\mathbb{R}$ models **magnitude with continuity** filling the gaps left by [rational numbers](./Rational Numbers.md) so that limits, calculus, and geometry behave smoothly

### Properties

- Ordered
- [closed](./Closed.md) under [addition](./Addition.md) and [multiplication](./Multiplication)
- Every [Cauchy sequence](./Cauchy Sequence.md) converges ([completeness](./Completeness.md))
- Forms a [field](./Field.md)

### Why Matters

$\mathbb{R}$ is the default scalar system for:

- Measurement
- Geometry
- Limits and analysis
- Real vector spaces $\mathbb{R}^n$

### Contrast

- $\mathbb{Q}$ lacks completeness
- $\mathbb{C}$ extends $\mathbb{R}$ to support solutions to polynomial equations
