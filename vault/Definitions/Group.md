## Definition

A `set` with 1 `operation` satisfying:
- Closure
- Associativity
- Identity element
- Inverses

If the operations is also `commutative` then it is an [[Abelian Group]]

## Key Idea

A group captures reversible combination

## Example

$(\mathbb{Z}, +)$
